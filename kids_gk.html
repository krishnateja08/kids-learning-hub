The Amazing Human Body
You are a walking miracle!
🧠

Brain

Control centre of your body. Has 86 billion nerve cells! Uses 20% of all your body's energy. Weighs about 1.4 kg.

❤️

Heart

Beats 100,000 times a day! Pumps 7,000 litres of blood daily. Size of your fist. Made of special muscle.

🫁

Lungs

We breathe 20,000 times a day! Right lung is bigger than left. They float on water — only organ that does!

🦷

Teeth

Children have 20 milk teeth. Adults have 32 teeth. Enamel is the hardest substance in the human body!

👁️

Eyes

Can see 10 million colours! Each eye has 130 million light receptor cells. Blink 10,000 times per day!

🦴

Bones

206 bones in an adult. Babies are born with 270 bones — they fuse as you grow! The femur is the longest bone.

🩸

Blood

5 litres of blood in your body. Red blood cells live 120 days. Blood has 4 types: A, B, AB, O.

👂

Ears

Help you hear AND balance! The smallest bones in the body (hammer, anvil, stirrup) are inside your ear!

🧬

DNA – Your Body's Blueprint!

Every cell in your body contains DNA — a set of instructions that makes you unique! If you stretched all the DNA from one human cell, it would be 2 metres long. All the DNA in your body would reach the Sun and back 600 times!

Fun with Science!

Electricity

Magic of Mathematics!
Maths is the language of the Universe!
0️⃣

Zero (0)

INVENTED IN INDIA by Aryabhatta! Without zero, computers and phones cannot work.

π

Pi (π)

Pi = 3.14159... Never-ending number! Used to calculate circles. Aryabhatta calculated it too!

🔢

Prime Numbers

Numbers with only 2 factors: 1 & itself. Examples: 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17, 19...

🌀

Fibonacci Numbers

Each number = sum of previous two: 0,1,1,2,3,5,8,13,21... Found in flowers, shells, nature!

📐

Pythagoras Theorem

a² + b² = c² for right-angled triangles. Used in building construction daily!

∞

Infinity

A concept of something without end. Numbers go on forever — there's no "biggest number!"

📊 Fun Maths Tricks
✖️

Multiply by 9 Trick

9×1=9, 9×2=18, ... digits always add to 9! (18→1+8=9, 27→2+7=9) Try it!

💫

The 11 Trick

11×12: put the digits 1 and 2 on sides, sum in middle → 1,3,2 = 132! (works up to 11×18)

🔁

Any Number × 0

Any number multiplied by ZERO is always ZERO! 1000 × 0 = 0!

🌟

Powers of 2

2,4,8,16,32,64... Double each time! Computers use this — everything is 0s and 1s!
