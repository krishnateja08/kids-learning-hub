1

Gather 10 different items from around your home.

2

4

Color Magic! Ages 5–6

Watch primary colors travel and BLEND into secondary colors using paper towels. Pure magic!

💧 Water🎨 Food Coloring🧻 Paper Towels🥛 3 Cups
1

Fill 3 cups: one red, one yellow, one empty in the middle.

2

Fold a paper towel and place between cup 1 and the middle.

3

Do the same between the middle cup and cup 3.

4

Wait 30–60 mins. Watch colors mix to form ORANGE! 🍊🎉

DIY Invisible Ink Ages 7–8

Write a secret message with lemon juice — invisible until heat reveals it! Spy science for real.

🍋 Lemon Juice✏️ Cotton Bud📄 White Paper💡 Lamp
1

Squeeze lemon juice into a small cup.

2

Dip a cotton bud and write your secret message on white paper.

3

Let it dry completely — it turns invisible!

4

Hold under a lamp (adult help) — message appears! 🕵️🔥

Build a Solar Oven Ages 9–10

Use the sun's energy to melt a marshmallow or make s'mores! Real renewable energy engineering at home.

📦 Pizza Box🪞 Foil🎁 Plastic Wrap🍫 Marshmallow/Chocolate
1

Cut a flap in the pizza box lid, leaving a 2cm border.

2

Cover the inside of the flap with aluminum foil (shiny side out) — this reflects sunlight in.

3

Cover the opening with plastic wrap to trap heat like a greenhouse.

4

Place your marshmallow inside, point the box at the sun, and wait 30–45 mins. It will melt! ☀️🍫
